FAQ

Ask questions about our plans and services

How long does it take to develop my website?

Development time varies according to the complexity of the project. On average, basic websites take 2-3 weeks, while more complex projects can take 6-8 weeks.

Do the prices include hosting and domain?

Yes, all our plans include hosting for 1 year and domain registration (.ie or .com). After the first year, you can renew these services with us or transfer them to another provider.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. The difference in value will be calculated in proportion to the remaining period of your current contract.
